# Residency — support

Residency is an iOS app that passively counts the days you spend in each country to
help you track tax-residency thresholds (like the 183-day rule), with all location
processing done on your device.

## Get help

For questions, bug reports, feature requests, or account/data deletion requests, email:

**damian.kimmelman@gmail.com**

We aim to respond within 2 business days.

## Common questions

**Does the app send my location anywhere?**
No. Coordinates are resolved to a country code on your device and never leave your
phone. See the [privacy policy](privacy) for details.

**The day count seems wrong.**
Days flagged as "inferred" cover tracking gaps (e.g. the phone was off). Open the day
detail view to review and correct any day manually.

**How do I delete my data?**
All data lives on your device — deleting the app deletes it. If you enabled sync,
you can delete your account and synced counts in Settings, or email us.
